FPV Drone Build

#Blog #Drone #FPV #DIY #Electronics

Building my first FPV (First Person View) drone was both an exciting and technically enriching experience. This project allowed me to apply practical engineering concepts while honing my hands-on skills in electronics, assembly, and flight configuration.

The build began with a 4-in-1 ESC, F4 flight controller, and VTX. Soldering was performed with precision, ensuring clean joints for signal and power reliability.

I used 2207 2450KV brushless motors powered by a 4S 1300mAh LiPo battery, secured with an XT60 connector and filtered using a low-ESR capacitor.
